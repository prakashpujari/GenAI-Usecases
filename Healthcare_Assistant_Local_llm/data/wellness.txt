Health and Wellness Guide

1. Nutrition
- Focus on whole foods: fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins
- Limit processed foods, refined sugars, and excessive saturated fats
- Stay hydrated and monitor portion sizes

2. Physical Activity
- Aim for 150 minutes of moderate exercise a week
- Include muscle-strengthening activities twice weekly
- Prioritize mobility and balance as you age

3. Mental Wellbeing
- Practice stress reduction: meditation, deep breathing, hobbies
- Ensure 7-9 hours of sleep per night
- Maintain social connections and seek help when needed

4. Preventive Care
- Routine check-ups and screenings
- Immunizations according to guidelines
- Healthy lifestyle counseling and support
