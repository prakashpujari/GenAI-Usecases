Treatment Guidelines

1. Type 2 Diabetes Treatment
- Lifestyle management: diet, exercise, weight loss
- First-line medication: Metformin
- GLP-1 receptor agonists for cardiovascular benefits
- Insulin therapy for advanced cases

2. Hypertension Treatment
- Lifestyle: sodium reduction, exercise, weight management
- First-line medications: ACE inhibitors, ARBs, thiazide diuretics
- Follow-up and titration based on blood pressure readings

3. Asthma Treatment
- Short-acting beta agonists for relief
- Inhaled corticosteroids for control
- Long-acting muscarinic antagonists as add-on therapy
- Action plan for exacerbations
